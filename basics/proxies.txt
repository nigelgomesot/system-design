# REF: https://medium.com/@rzkmak/forward-vs-reverse-proxy-f97729cdb86e

Proxy server:
device which acts on behalf of another device.

2 types:
1) Forward Proxy / Proxy:
- acts on behalf of clients
- used to mask client device identity(ip address / location)
- eg: VPNs

2) Reverse Proxy:
- acts on behalf of servers
- used to mask server device identity(ip address / location)
- e.g: nginx (load balancers, caching, compression)
